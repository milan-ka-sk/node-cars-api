Cars API

RESTfull Endpoints:

  • Get all cars:

    GET: https://mk-cars-api.herokuapp.com/cars

  • Get a car with a particular ID:

    GET: https://mk-cars-api.herokuapp.com/cars/<id>

  • Add a car:

    POST: https://mk-cars-api.herokuapp.com/cars;

  • Update a car:

    PUT: https://mk-cars-api.herokuapp.com/cars/<id>

  • Delete a car with a particular ID:

    DELETE: https://mk-cars-api.herokuapp.com/cars/<id>
